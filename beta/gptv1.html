About

Professor at Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) with over 18 years of teaching and research experience. Director of the Centre for Technical Support (CTS), leading campus-wide infrastructure, software systems, and strategic technology initiatives.

Research interests include Data Science, Deep Learning, Cyber Security, Language Modeling, and Web 3.0. Guided multiple PhD scholars and contributed to institutional digital platforms including VTOP.

Actively conducts faculty development programs, workshops, and training sessions on AI, Machine Learning, Full-Stack Development, Research Tools, and Emerging Technologies.
