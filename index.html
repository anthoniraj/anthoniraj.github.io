Anthoniraj Amalanathan is a Professor at Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) in the School of Computer Science and Engineering. He is also the Director of Centre for Technical Support (SDC). He has over 18 years of experience in teaching courses like Python, Java, C, C++, and Data Science. Prof. Anthoniraj's research focuses on Data Science, Deep Learning, Cyber Security, Language Modeling, and Web 3.0.
He has guided several PhD students and has strong technical skills in AI, Programming, full-stack development, and system administration. He manages teams at the Software Development Cell, developing VIT's in-house software portal. Prof. Anthoniraj is also active in various university committees and has published several research papers in fields like deep learning, recommendation systems, and data science.
He regularly trains faculty members and students on topics like AI and Machine Leaning, web development, mobile app development, research tools, and Data Science.
