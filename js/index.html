About Me

I am a 2017-2018 Dissertation Fellow at the University of Connecticut Humanities Institute and am currently completing my dissertation, “Reclaiming Moral Agency: What Sufferers Owe Themselves.” I did my undergraduate work at SUNY Stony Brook and will receive my PhD. from the University of Connecticut in Spring 2018. My research is in Ethics, Moral Psychology, and Feminist Philosophy with influences from Non-Western Philosophy and Existentialism.
